MISSION STATEMENT

We are a non-governmental organization (NGO) dedicated to promoting menstrual health and empowering women through education, access to sanitary pads, and comprehensive support. Our mission is to break the barriers created by menstrual taboos and empower women to manage their menstrual health with dignity, thereby enhancing their overall well-being and enabling them to reach their full potential

REASONS OF EXISTENCE

This NGO focused on sharing pads and educating women on menstruation was brought up to address knowledge gaps, improve access to menstrual products, empower women, challenge taboos, and advocate for policy changes. It promotes menstrual health, dignity, and equality for women and girls in society.

WHAT WE DO

To accomplish the mission, the NGO can conduct educational workshops, distribute pads through partnerships and donation programs, engage in community awareness campaigns, collaborate with schools, provide mobile clinics, offer women's empowerment programs, advocate for policy changes, and establish monitoring and evaluation systems. These multifaceted approaches will ensure comprehensive menstrual health education, access to sanitary pads, empowerment of women, and systemic change towards breaking taboos and promoting menstrual health and dignity.
